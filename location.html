ADCS 2004 will be held in the

ICT Building,
111 Barry Street,
University of Melbourne,
Carlton, VIC 3053.

The University of Melbourne is located within easy walking distance of the many facilities and attractions offered by downtown Melbourne.

Here is a University map. The ICT Building is Building 105, at grid location P14.

Accommodation

There are two nearby hotels, both within easy walking distance of both the University and the CBD:
  • The Ibis Hotel Melbourne, which has advised of room-only accommodation for $AUD99 per night, inclusive of GST, at the "Government/University" rate. (Note -- there are two Ibis hotels in Melbourne, the correct one is the one linked above, at 15-21 Therry Street, Melbourne 3000.)
  • The Rydges Carlton, which has advised of room-only accommodation for $AUD135 per night, inclusive of GST, at the "Government/University" rate.
There is no guaranteed quota of rooms at these rates, and you should make you own bookings directly with the hotels in accordance with your own needs, via the fax/telephone numbers listed on their web pages. Note also that the ADCS organisers have no financial relationship of any type with either venue, and you are free to choose any accommodation options -- including using Internet booking rates if they are cheaper.

Other nearby accommodation includes Toad Hall (budget-priced).
