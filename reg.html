Registration

The registration fee is $176 (discounted to $132 for full-time students). Both prices include GST and cover attendance at all sessions; an informal dinner on the evening of Sunday 12 December; morning and afternoon refreshments on 13 December; and a copy of the Symposium proceedings. To be eligible for the discount, student registrants must be studying full-time in an Australasian tertiary institution.

All registration payments must be made by credit card. No other payment options are available (sorry). A registration and payment form (PDF document) is linked here. Please print the form, complete your registration and credit card details, and fax/post it back.

Once we have processed your payment, a confirmation note and receipt will be emailed back to you.

Bulk registration rates are available if you have more than four people attending from your institution. Email alistair / csse.unimelb.edu.au to find out more.

At least one author of each accepted paper or poster is required to register at the time they lodge their final camera-ready copy.

Pre-registrations will not be accepted after 6 December, 2004. After that date, you will be required to register on the morning of the symposium. A late fee may be applied.
